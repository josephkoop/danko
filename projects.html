Tire Recycling Plant

Across the entirety of Belize, used tires have been going to waste and are being left to rot in dumps with no further value. Our tire recycling plant is a solution to this issue. We take used tires and bring them to our plant, where they get processed into recycled oil. The byproducts get disposed of and the recycled oil gets sold to other companies. This provides industries with another fuel source while decreasing the amount of waste in the environment.
